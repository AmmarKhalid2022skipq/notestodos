Core Features

Designed for clarity. Built for speed. SmartApp replaces cluttered to-do lists with structured, visual workflows.

Interactive Kanban

Visualize your workflow. Drag and drop tasks effortlessly between "To Do", "In Progress", and "Done". Keep track of priority flags and exact due dates.

Rich-Text Notes

Capture your thoughts beautifully with our integrated Quill.js editor. Format text with bolding, italics, lists, and colors, ready whenever inspiration strikes.

Priority Matrix

Focus on what actually matters. Our Eisenhower Box automatically separates tasks that are "Urgent & Important" from those you just need to "Schedule" for later.
